About Me

Exploring the convergence of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to build the next generation of autonomous defense systems.

I'm currently pursuing my M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Patna with a CPI of 9.63/10, specializing in AI-driven cybersecurity research. My work focuses on adversarial machine learning, reinforcement learning for autonomous cyber defense, and data-driven threat intelligence.

My research journey includes contributing to satellite cybersecurity at ISRO – ISTRAC, national cyber threat analysis at the Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (Ministry of Home Affairs), and machine learning-based intrusion detection research at IIIT Naya Raipur.

I'm passionate about developing explainable, production-ready ML approaches for securing critical infrastructure and large-scale systems. My research interests center on creating intelligent systems that can autonomously detect, analyze, and respond to evolving cyber threats.

Beyond research, I actively contribute to the tech community through event organization, academic leadership roles, and continuous learning. I was selected for the Amazon ML Summer School 2025 and have been recognized in multiple hackathons and coding competitions.
